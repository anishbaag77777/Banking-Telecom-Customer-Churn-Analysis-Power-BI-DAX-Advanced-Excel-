# Banking-Telecom-Customer-Churn-Analysis-Power-BI-DAX-Advanced-Excel-

##  Project Overview

Customer churn means customers stop using a company's services. Losing customers affects business growth and revenue.

This project analyzes customer data from the Banking and Telecom industries to identify why customers leave and to build machine learning models that predict which customers are likely to churn.

# Project Objectives

## 1. Identify Key Churn Drivers

Analyze the factors that contribute to customer churn.

Study customer demographics, account details, and transactional behavior.

Identify patterns that differentiate churned customers from retained customers.

## 2. Customer Segmentation
   
Segment customers based on demographics, account information, and customer activity.

Identify high-risk, loyal, and high-value customer groups.

Compare churn rates across different customer segments.

## 3. Predict Customer Churn

Build machine learning models to predict customers likely to churn.

Compare multiple algorithms and select the best-performing model.

Evaluate feature importance to understand the factors affecting churn.

## 4. Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis

Estimate the lifetime value of different customer segments.

Analyze the impact of churn on business revenue.

Identify high-value customers requiring focused retention efforts.

## 5. Retention Strategy Recommendations

Recommend personalized retention strategies based on customer behavior.

Improve customer engagement through targeted offers and services.

Reduce churn by focusing on high-risk customer groups.

## 6. Continuous Monitoring

Monitor churn trends over time.

Update predictive models using new customer data.

Track the effectiveness of retention strategies and business performance.
